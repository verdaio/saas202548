## Thumbnail Style

### Layout Principles

1. **Face Rule**: Include a face when possible (increases CTR)
2. **Text Rule**: Maximum 4 words, high contrast
3. **Focal Point**: One clear subject, not cluttered
4. **Consistency**: Recognizable as your brand at a glance

### Thumbnail Template

| Element | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| Dimensions | 1280 x 720 pixels |
| Format | PNG (preferred) or JPG |
| Text area | Right 1/3 or bottom 1/3 |
| Face position | Left or center |
| Background | Solid, gradient, or blurred |

### Text on Thumbnails

- Font: Primary font, Bold weight
- Size: Large enough to read at small sizes
- Colors: High contrast with background
- Outline/shadow: Optional, for legibility
- Style: Consistent across all thumbnails

### Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Use bold, readable text
- Include expressive faces
- Create visual contrast
- Keep it simple

**Don't:**
- Use small or thin fonts
- Overcrowd with elements
- Use misleading images
- Copy other creators exactly
