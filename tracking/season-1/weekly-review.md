# Season 1 Weekly Review Template

**Purpose:** Maintain weekly ritual to ensure plan adherence, track metrics, and make one-change-per-week decisions
**Frequency:** Weekly (recommended: Monday)
**Duration:** 30–60 minutes

---

Copy this template for each week's review:

#### 3. Decision Policy (One Change Per Week)

**LOCKED:** Change only **one major variable** per week to isolate impact.

**Decision Matrix:**
- [ ] **Strong retention + weak CTR** → Change **thumbnail** first
- [ ] **Weak retention early** → Change **hook structure** (not packaging)
- [ ] **Both weak** → Adjust topic framing/angle (next episode)

**Decision for This Week:**
- Change: [e.g., "Update thumbnail for EP002" or "Adjust hook structure for EP003"]
- Rationale: [Why this change based on metrics]

**Changes to Avoid This Week:**
- Do NOT change: [List what you're NOT changing to maintain isolation]
