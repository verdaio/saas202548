## Section Guidelines

### Summary (First 150 Characters Most Important)

YouTube shows only the first 150 characters before "Show more." Make them count.

**Template:**
> In this video, I [action verb] [topic]. You'll learn [key outcome 1] and [key outcome 2].

**Example:**
> In this video, I build a complete authentication system from scratch. You'll learn JWT tokens, secure password hashing, and refresh token rotation.

### Timestamps (Chapters)

- Required for videos over 5 minutes
- First timestamp must be `0:00`
- Minimum 3 timestamps
- Each segment should be at least 10 seconds
- Use consistent formatting

### Resources Mentioned

- Only include resources actually discussed in video
- Use full URLs (not shorteners) for transparency
- Group by type if many resources

### Related Videos

- Link to 2-3 relevant videos from your channel
- Increases watch time and engagement
- Use full YouTube URLs

### Disclaimers

**Affiliate Links:**
> Some links above are affiliate links, meaning I may earn a commission at no extra cost to you if you make a purchase.

**Not Professional Advice:**
> This video is for educational purposes only and does not constitute [financial/legal/medical] advice. Consult a professional for your specific situation.

**Opinions:**
> The opinions expressed are my own and do not represent any employer or organization.

### Hashtags

- Maximum 15 hashtags (YouTube may ignore excess)
- Place at end of description
- Use a mix of broad and specific tags
- Don't stuff irrelevant hashtags

---

## SEO Tips

1. **Front-load keywords**: Put important terms in first 150 characters
2. **Natural language**: Write for humans first, algorithms second
3. **Include search terms**: What would someone search to find this?
4. **Update old descriptions**: Refresh with new links, timestamps
5. **Consistent structure**: Helps returning viewers navigate
