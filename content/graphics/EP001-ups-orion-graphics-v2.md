## Production Notes (MVP)

### Simplification Strategy:
- **Use built-in tools:** PowerPoint/Keynote have sufficient animation capabilities for MVP
- **Avoid complex software:** No need for After Effects at MVP stage
- **Static + simple motion:** Slides/pans/fades are enough; avoid particle effects or 3D

### Color Palette (Consistent Across All Graphics):
- **Primary:** UPS Brown (#351C15), UPS Gold (#FFB500)
- **Supporting:** White (#FFFFFF), Dark Gray (#2C2C2C), Off-white (#F5F5F5)
- **Accent:** Green (#34A853) for "good/optimal", Red (#EA4335) for "bad/inefficient"

### Typography:
- **Headings:** Bold sans-serif (Arial Bold, Helvetica Bold)
- **Body:** Regular sans-serif (Arial, Helvetica)
- **Numbers:** Tabular figures (monospace when showing data)

### Export Settings:
- **Resolution:** 1920×1080 (1080p)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps (sufficient for simple animations)
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264) OR PNG sequence (if layering in editor)
